FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WebStudio?
WebStudio is a platform where users can download professionally designed website templates to quickly build their own websites. Our templates are responsive, modern, and easy to customize.
Are the templates free?
We offer both free and premium templates. Free templates can be downloaded instantly, while premium templates require a one-time payment.
Can I use WebStudio templates for commercial projects?
Yes. You can use our templates for personal and commercial projects unless stated otherwise in the license.
Do I need coding knowledge to use the templates?
Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is helpful. However, our templates are structured clearly, making them beginner-friendly.
Are the templates mobile responsive?
Yes. All WebStudio templates are fully responsive and work smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
Can I customize the templates?
Absolutely. You can edit colors, fonts, images, content, and layout sections. The templates are built to be easily customizable.
Will I receive updates after purchasing a template?
Yes. Premium users receive updates and improvements whenever they are available.
How do I download a template?
Simply choose a template, click Download, extract the ZIP file, and open the index.html file in your browser to start using it.
Do you provide support?
Yes. Basic support is provided for installation issues, setup guidance, and minor template-related questions.
Can I resell WebStudio templates?
No. Reselling or redistributing our templates as your own product is strictly prohibited.
